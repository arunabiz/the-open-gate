About Us

The Open Gate was initiated in October 2019 by a group of friends in the intention of providing a service to the society and since then TOG has been able to extend our support to the ones in need.

TOG has successfully completed a few projects over the last two years such as, helping to build a good educational environment for rural schools and helping with the educational needs of students of rural areas by providing them with stationeries and seminars to support their education.

TOG also conducts educational programs, career guidance programs and skill development programs to the youth. During the pandemic TOG has extended its support to the people who were affected by the pandemic and were struggling to find the basic needs to feed their families, by providing them with food packs and essential items. TOG hopes to extend its helping hands to a larger community in the years to come with the support of everyone in the community.
